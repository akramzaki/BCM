PETRONAS Resiliency Model was introduced in 2011 which completes the approach to managing risk in PETRONAS.

ERM

ERM is a structured and holistic approach to identify, assess, treat and monitor risks. REDUCE likelihood and impact of all identified risks

CM

CM is a comprehensive set of processes that aims to prepare the organization. RESPOND to immediate risk events

BCM

BCM is a holistic management process that aims to build the capability of an organization. RECOVER from prolonged disruption to meet business obligations
