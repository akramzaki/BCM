Business Impact Analysis

1) Identifies Critical Business Functions, assesses the impact of unavailability of these functions over time

2) sets prioritized timeframes for resuming these functions and

3) specifies Minimum Resource Requirements to be allocated to recover and resume these functions.

The completed BIA shall be approved by respective approving authority.

Development of BIA involved 3 main phases

3 main phases

The business functions are categorized based on its Recovery Time Objective (RTO) and magnitude of its impact to business

Business Functions will be categorized into either one of the following priority based on its Recovery Time Objective (RTO):

Priority 1: These are functions that need to be resumed immediately irrespective of disaster within 24 hours to 2 Weeks.

Priority 2: These are functions that can be resumed within 1 month from disaster

The CBF under priority 1 will be resumed in phases based on their RTO and impact
BIA is intended to identify business functions, and their respective criticality and RTO
MRR and their required details in supporting CBF are identified in BIA

MRR : Minimum level of resources required to resume CBF.
